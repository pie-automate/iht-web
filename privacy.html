Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This policy explains what information the Indie Hacker Tycoon mobile app ("the app", "we") collects and how it's used. The app is a single-player game. It has no accounts, no login, and no social features.

Information we collect

  • Anonymous usage analytics. We use Mixpanel to collect anonymous, aggregated gameplay events (for example: a career started, a month advanced, a milestone reached) so we can understand how the game is played and improve it. This is tied to a random analytics identifier, not to your name, email, or contacts.
  • Basic device/technical data that the analytics service collects automatically, such as device model, operating-system version, and general region. This helps us fix bugs and support different devices.

We do not ask for or collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or any other personal information. We do not require you to create an account.

Your game data

Your career save is stored locally on your device. It is not uploaded to us or to any server. Deleting the app deletes your save.

How we use the information

Only to operate, debug, and improve the game (understanding feature usage, difficulty balance, crashes, and retention). We do not use it for advertising profiles.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. Anonymous analytics data is processed by our analytics provider (Mixpanel) on our behalf. We may disclose information if required by law.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Your choices

The data collected is anonymous and not linked to you. You can stop all collection at any time by uninstalling the app. Future versions may add an in-app analytics opt-out.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves (for example, if we add purchases or ads in a future version). Material changes will be reflected here with a new "last updated" date.
